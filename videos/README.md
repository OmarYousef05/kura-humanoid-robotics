## Publication checklist

Before adding a thumbnail, file, or link:

- Confirm that the media is approved for public portfolio use.
- Check the full frame for private code, terminal output, credentials, logs, and configuration.
- Confirm that team members and third parties are represented appropriately.
- Use an accurate description and avoid unverified claims or competition results.
- Link to the published media rather than committing unnecessarily large video files.

All team-related footage and visual material remains the property of its respective owner.
